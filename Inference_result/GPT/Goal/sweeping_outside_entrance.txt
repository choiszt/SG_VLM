Action Planning for Sweeping Outside Entrance:

1. Gather necessary cleaning supplies such as a broom, dustpan, and outdoor brush.
2. Put on appropriate protective gear such as gloves and a mask.
3. Start by removing any loose debris or leaves from the entrance area using the broom.
4. Sweep the entire outside entrance, making sure to reach all corners and edges.
5. Use the outdoor brush to scrub away any stubborn dirt or stains on the floor.
6. Collect the swept dirt and debris into the dustpan and dispose of it properly.
7. Finally, give the entrance area a quick inspection to ensure it is clean and tidy before finishing the task.
