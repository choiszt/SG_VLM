Action Planning for Making Chocolate Spread:

1. Gather ingredients: 
   - 1 cup of chocolate chips
   - 1/2 cup of sweetened condensed milk
   - 1/4 cup of unsalted butter

2. Melt the chocolate chips: 
   - Place the chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl.
   - Microwave on high for 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.

3. Heat the sweetened condensed milk and butter: 
   - In a small saucepan, combine the sweetened condensed milk and unsalted butter.
   - Heat over low heat, stirring constantly, until the butter is melted and the mixture is well combined.

4. Combine the melted chocolate and milk mixture: 
   - Pour the melted chocolate into the saucepan with the milk mixture.
   - Stir well until the ingredients are fully combined and smooth.

5. Let it cool: 
   - Allow the chocolate spread to cool at room temperature for 10-15 minutes.

6. Transfer to a jar: 
   - Carefully pour the chocolate spread into a clean, airtight jar or container.

7. Store and enjoy: 
   - Seal the jar tightly and store it in the refrigerator.
   - The chocolate spread can be enjoyed on toast, pancakes, or as a filling for pastries.
