Action Plan for making iced chocolate:

1. Gather the ingredients: chocolate powder, sugar, milk, ice cubes.
2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
3. In a separate container, mix chocolate powder and sugar.
4. Heat a small amount of milk in a microwave or on a stove.
5. Pour the hot milk into the container with the chocolate powder and sugar mixture. Stir well until the powder is dissolved.
6. Add the remaining cold milk to the mixture and stir again.
7. Pour the chocolate milk over the ice cubes in the glass and stir gently. Enjoy your iced chocolate!

Note: You may adjust the amount of chocolate powder, sugar, and milk according to your taste preferences.
