Action Plan for Clearing Table After Coffee:

1. Gather necessary cleaning supplies such as a damp cloth, a sponge, and a cleaning spray.
2. Remove any leftover coffee cups or mugs from the table and place them in the sink or dishwasher.
3. Take a damp cloth and wipe down the table surface to remove any spills or coffee stains.
4. Use the sponge and cleaning spray to clean any stubborn stains or residue on the table.
5. Collect any used napkins or paper towels and dispose of them in the trash.
6. Inspect the table for any remaining debris or clutter and remove it.
7. Finally, double-check the cleanliness of the table and ensure that it is ready for the next use.

Note: Make sure to follow proper safety guidelines while handling cleaning supplies and disposing of waste.
