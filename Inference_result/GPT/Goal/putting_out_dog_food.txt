Planning for putting out dog food:
1. Go to the storage area where the dog food is kept.
2. Pick up the bag or container of dog food.
3. Walk to the designated area where the dog food bowl is placed.
4. Open the bag or container of dog food.
5. Pour the desired amount of dog food into the dog food bowl.
6. Close the bag or container of dog food.
7. Return to your original location.
