Sure! Here is a simple 7-step action plan to make a blended iced cappuccino:

1. Gather the ingredients:
   - 1 cup of strong brewed coffee
   - 1 cup of milk
   - 2 tablespoons of sugar
   - 1 cup of ice cubes

2. Pour the coffee into a blender.

3. Add the milk to the blender.

4. Add the sugar to the blender.

5. Add the ice cubes to the blender.

6. Blend all the ingredients together on high speed until smooth and frothy.

7. Pour the blended mixture into a glass and enjoy your homemade blended iced cappuccino!

Remember to adjust the quantities of the ingredients according to your taste preferences. Enjoy!
