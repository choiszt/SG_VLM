Action Planning for "prepare_sea_salt_soak":

1. Gather ingredients: sea salt and warm water.
2. Fill a basin or tub with warm water.
3. Add sea salt to the water, approximately 1-2 cups depending on the size of the basin or tub.
4. Stir the water gently to dissolve the sea salt.
5. Test the water temperature to ensure it is comfortable for soaking.
6. Once the water temperature is suitable, soak the desired body parts in the sea salt soak for 15-20 minutes.
7. After the soak, dry off and moisturize your skin if desired.
