Sure! Here is a simple action plan for cooking brussels sprouts:

1. Gather all the ingredients needed for cooking brussels sprouts, such as brussels sprouts, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit (200 degrees Celsius).
3. Wash the brussels sprouts thoroughly and remove any outer leaves that are discolored or damaged.
4. Cut the brussels sprouts in half and place them on a baking sheet.
5. Drizzle olive oil over the brussels sprouts and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and roast the brussels sprouts for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly browned.
7. Once cooked, remove the brussels sprouts from the oven and serve them as a side dish or add them to your favorite recipes.

Remember to always exercise caution when using kitchen appliances or working with hot surfaces. Enjoy your cooked brussels sprouts!
