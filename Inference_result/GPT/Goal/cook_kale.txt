Action Planning for Cooking Kale:

1. Gather ingredients: Kale leaves, olive oil, salt, and any additional desired seasonings.
2. Wash the kale leaves thoroughly under cold water.
3. Remove the tough stems from the kale leaves and discard them.
4. Chop the kale leaves into bite-sized pieces.
5. Heat a pan or skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of olive oil.
6. Add the chopped kale leaves to the pan and saut√© them for a few minutes until they become wilted and tender.
7. Season the cooked kale with salt and any additional desired seasonings before serving.

Note: You can customize this basic plan by adding other ingredients or flavors as per your preference.
