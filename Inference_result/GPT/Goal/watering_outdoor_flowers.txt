Action Planning for watering_outdoor_flowers:
1. Check the weather forecast to ensure it's not going to rain in the next few hours.
2. Gather necessary supplies such as a watering can or hose, and fill it with water.
3. Put on appropriate outdoor clothing and shoes.
4. Locate the outdoor flower area and assess which flowers need watering.
5. Begin watering the flowers, starting from one end and moving systematically to cover all areas.
6. Ensure each flower receives an adequate amount of water, taking care not to overwater or underwater.
7. After watering, check the soil moisture levels and adjust if necessary. Store the watering supplies in a designated area for future use.
