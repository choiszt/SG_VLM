1. Gather necessary equipment: Chlorine tablets, chlorine dispenser, pool water testing kit, and protective gloves.
2. Test the pool water to determine the current chlorine levels.
3. Calculate the amount of chlorine tablets needed based on the pool size and current chlorine levels.
4. Open the chlorine dispenser and place the required number of chlorine tablets inside.
5. Attach the chlorine dispenser to a pool skimmer or floater.
6. Place the chlorine dispenser in the pool, ensuring it is evenly distributed.
7. Monitor the pool's chlorine levels over time and adjust the chlorine tablets as needed to maintain the desired chlorine concentration.
