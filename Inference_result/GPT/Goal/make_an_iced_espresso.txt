Action Planning for making an iced espresso:

1. Gather the necessary ingredients: espresso coffee, ice cubes, milk (optional), and sweetener (optional).
2. Brew a strong shot of espresso using an espresso machine or a coffee maker.
3. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
4. Pour the freshly brewed espresso over the ice cubes.
5. Add milk to the desired level, if preferred.
6. Stir the mixture gently to combine the ingredients.
7. Add sweetener, such as sugar or syrup, if desired. Stir again.

Enjoy your homemade iced espresso!
