Action Planning for Taking Clothes out of Washer:

1. Locate the laundry room.
2. Open the washer lid or door.
3. Remove any excess water by draining it or using a towel to soak it up.
4. Take out the clothes from the washer and place them into a laundry basket or a clean surface.
5. Close the washer lid or door.
6. Check if any clothes are stuck or left behind in the washer and remove them if necessary.
7. Carry the laundry basket or clothes to the desired location for further processing, such as drying or folding.

Note: Make sure to follow any specific instructions or safety precautions provided by the washer's manufacturer.
