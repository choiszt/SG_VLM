Action Planning for Making a Steak:

1. Gather the necessary ingredients and tools, including a steak, salt, pepper, olive oil, and a frying pan.
2. Preheat the frying pan on medium-high heat.
3. Season both sides of the steak with salt and pepper.
4. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil onto the preheated frying pan.
5. Place the steak onto the frying pan and cook it for a few minutes on each side, depending on your desired level of doneness.
6. Remove the steak from the frying pan and let it rest for a few minutes to allow the juices to redistribute.
7. Serve the steak and enjoy your delicious meal!

Note: The cooking time and temperature may vary depending on the thickness of the steak and your preferred level of doneness.
