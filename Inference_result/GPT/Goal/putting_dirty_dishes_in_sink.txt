Action Planning for putting_dirty_dishes_in_sink:

1. Pick up dirty dishes from the dining table.
2. Carry the dishes to the kitchen sink.
3. Turn on the tap to fill the sink with water.
4. Place the dirty dishes into the sink.
5. Add dishwashing liquid or soap to the sink.
6. Use a sponge or brush to scrub the dishes clean.
7. Rinse off the soap from the dishes and let them air dry.

Note: If there are too many dishes to fit in the sink, you may need to repeat steps 3-7 in batches until all the dishes are cleaned.
