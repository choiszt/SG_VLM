Action Planning for Fertilizing Garden:

1. Gather necessary gardening tools and materials such as fertilizer, watering can, and gardening gloves.
2. Put on the gardening gloves to protect your hands.
3. Measure the appropriate amount of fertilizer according to the instructions on the packaging.
4. Spread the fertilizer evenly throughout the garden, focusing on areas where plants need more nutrients.
5. Water the garden using the watering can to help the fertilizer soak into the soil.
6. Clean up any spills or excess fertilizer to avoid harming the plants.
7. Store the gardening tools and materials in their designated place for future use.

Note: Remember to follow any specific instructions provided by the manufacturer of the fertilizer and consider the needs of different plants in your garden.
