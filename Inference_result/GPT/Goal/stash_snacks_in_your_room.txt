Action Plan for Stashing Snacks in Your Room:

1. Gather all the snacks you want to stash in your room.
2. Find a suitable storage container or drawer to keep the snacks in.
3. Clean and organize the storage container or drawer, if needed.
4. Place the snacks neatly inside the storage container or drawer.
5. Close the storage container or drawer securely to keep the snacks fresh and protected.
6. Choose a hidden or inconspicuous spot in your room to place the storage container or drawer.
7. Place the storage container or drawer in the chosen spot, ensuring it is easily accessible for you.

Remember to consider the shelf life and proper storage conditions for each snack to maintain their quality.
