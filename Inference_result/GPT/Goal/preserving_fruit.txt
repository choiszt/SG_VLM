1. Gather the necessary supplies, including airtight containers, fruit, and a cutting board.
2. Wash the fruit thoroughly to remove any dirt or bacteria.
3. Cut the fruit into desired sizes or slices, removing any pits, seeds, or stems.
4. Place the cut fruit into the airtight containers, leaving some space at the top for expansion.
5. Seal the containers tightly to prevent any air from entering.
6. Label the containers with the type of fruit and the date of preservation.
7. Store the containers in a cool, dry place, such as a refrigerator or pantry, to extend the shelf life of the fruit.
