Action Planning for cleaning a nickel:

1. Gather necessary cleaning supplies such as a soft cloth, mild soap, and warm water.
2. Wet the cloth with warm water and add a small amount of mild soap.
3. Gently scrub the nickel with the soapy cloth, making sure to remove any dirt or grime.
4. Rinse the nickel with clean water to remove any soap residue.
5. Dry the nickel thoroughly with a clean, dry cloth to prevent water spots.
6. Inspect the nickel for any remaining dirt or stains. If necessary, repeat steps 3-5.
7. Once the nickel is clean and dry, store it in a safe place to prevent further tarnishing or damage.
