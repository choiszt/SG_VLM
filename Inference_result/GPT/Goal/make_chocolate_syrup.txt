Sure! Here is a simple action plan to make chocolate syrup:

1. Gather the ingredients: cocoa powder, sugar, water.
2. In a small saucepan, combine 1/2 cup of cocoa powder and 1 cup of sugar.
3. Gradually stir in 1 cup of water until the mixture is smooth.
4. Place the saucepan on the stove over medium heat.
5. Bring the mixture to a boil, stirring constantly.
6. Once it boils, reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 5 minutes, continuing to stir.
7. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the chocolate syrup cool before using or storing it in a jar.

Please note that these steps are just a general guideline. Make sure to follow any specific instructions on the ingredients or recipe you are using.
