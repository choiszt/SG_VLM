Planning for cleaning a backpack:

1. Empty the contents of the backpack.
2. Shake out any loose dirt or debris from the backpack.
3. Fill a sink or basin with warm water and add a small amount of mild detergent.
4. Use a soft brush or cloth to gently scrub the inside and outside of the backpack.
5. Rinse the backpack thoroughly with clean water.
6. Hang the backpack to dry in a well-ventilated area.
7. Once the backpack is completely dry, reorganize and refill it with the desired items.
