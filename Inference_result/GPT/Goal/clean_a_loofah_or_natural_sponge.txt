Action Planning to Clean a Loofah or Natural Sponge:

1. Fill a basin or sink with warm water.
2. Add a small amount of mild liquid soap or dishwashing detergent to the water.
3. Place the loofah or natural sponge into the soapy water and let it soak for a few minutes.
4. Use your hands to gently squeeze and massage the loofah or sponge to remove any dirt or debris.
5. Rinse the loofah or sponge under running water to remove the soap.
6. Squeeze out any excess water from the loofah or sponge.
7. Hang the loofah or sponge in a well-ventilated area to air dry completely before using it again.
