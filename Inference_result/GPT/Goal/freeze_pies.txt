1. Check if you have any pies in the freezer.
2. If you have pies in the freezer, take them out and place them on a baking sheet.
3. Preheat the oven to the recommended temperature for baking the pies.
4. Once the oven is preheated, place the pies inside and set the timer for the recommended baking time.
5. While the pies are baking, clean up any mess or organize the kitchen if needed.
6. When the timer goes off, carefully remove the pies from the oven using oven mitts.
7. Allow the pies to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your freshly baked pies!
