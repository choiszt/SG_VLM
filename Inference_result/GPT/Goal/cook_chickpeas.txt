Action Planning to Cook Chickpeas:

1. Soak the chickpeas overnight in water to soften them.
2. Drain the soaked chickpeas and rinse them thoroughly.
3. Place the chickpeas in a large pot and add enough water to cover them.
4. Bring the water to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer.
5. Cook the chickpeas for 1 to 1.5 hours or until they are tender.
6. Once cooked, drain the chickpeas and rinse them with cold water to stop the cooking process.
7. Your chickpeas are now ready to be used in various recipes or as a snack. Enjoy!
