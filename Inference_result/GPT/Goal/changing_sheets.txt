Action Planning for Changing Sheets:

1. Gather clean sheets and pillowcases.
2. Remove the dirty sheets from the bed.
3. Launder the dirty sheets.
4. Make sure the mattress is clean and free of debris.
5. Place the fitted sheet onto the mattress, ensuring it is securely tucked in.
6. Add the flat sheet on top of the fitted sheet, making sure it is evenly spread.
7. Place the pillowcases on the pillows and arrange them neatly on the bed.

Note: This action plan assumes that you have the necessary cleaning supplies and access to a washing machine.
