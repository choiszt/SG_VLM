Sure! Here is a simple 7-step action plan to make baked pears:

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Wash and peel 4 ripe pears.
3. Cut the pears in half lengthwise and remove the core with a spoon.
4. Place the pear halves in a baking dish, cut side up.
5. Drizzle the pear halves with honey or maple syrup for added sweetness.
6. Sprinkle cinnamon or nutmeg over the pears for flavor.
7. Bake the pears in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly golden.

Enjoy your homemade baked pears!
