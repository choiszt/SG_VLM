1. Check if you have any fresh fruit available.
2. Sort and wash the fruit to remove any dirt or debris.
3. Cut the fruit into smaller, bite-sized pieces if needed.
4. Place the fruit on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
5. Place the baking sheet with the fruit in the freezer and let it freeze for a few hours or overnight.
6. Once the fruit is completely frozen, transfer it into airtight containers or freezer bags for storage.
7. Label the containers with the type of fruit and the date of freezing for easy identification.
