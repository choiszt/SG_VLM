Planning for carrying out garden furniture:

1. Gather necessary tools and equipment such as a screwdriver, wrench, and gloves.
2. Identify the location where the garden furniture needs to be placed.
3. Clear the area of any obstacles or debris.
4. Assemble the furniture according to the provided instructions.
5. Ensure that all the screws and bolts are tightened properly.
6. Lift and carry the assembled furniture to the designated location in the garden.
7. Place the furniture securely in the desired spot, ensuring stability and comfort.

Note: If the garden furniture is too heavy or requires multiple people to carry, please seek assistance to ensure safety.
