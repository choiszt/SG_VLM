Action Planning for buying basic garden tools:

1. Make a list of the basic garden tools you need.
2. Research different stores or online platforms that sell garden tools.
3. Compare prices and read reviews to find the best options.
4. Choose the store or platform where you want to make the purchase.
5. Check if there are any ongoing sales or discounts on the selected items.
6. Add the selected garden tools to your shopping cart or create a shopping list.
7. Proceed to checkout and complete the purchase.
