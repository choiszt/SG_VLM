1. Gather necessary tools: a drill, screws, a stud finder, and a bike wall mount.
2. Use the stud finder to locate a stud in the wall where you want to hang the bike.
3. Mark the location of the stud with a pencil.
4. Hold the bike wall mount against the wall at the marked location and use the drill to screw it into the stud.
5. Ensure the bike wall mount is securely attached to the wall.
6. Lift the bike and hang it on the bike wall mount, ensuring it is properly balanced and stable.
7. Test the stability by gently pushing and pulling the bike to ensure it is securely hung on the wall.
