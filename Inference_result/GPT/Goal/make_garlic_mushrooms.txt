Planning for making garlic mushrooms:

1. Gather the ingredients: mushrooms, garlic, butter, salt, and pepper.
2. Clean the mushrooms by wiping them with a damp cloth to remove any dirt.
3. Slice the mushrooms into thin slices.
4. Mince the garlic cloves.
5. Heat a pan over medium heat and melt the butter.
6. Add the minced garlic to the pan and saut√© until fragrant.
7. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pan and cook until they are soft and golden brown.
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
9. Serve the garlic mushrooms as a side dish or as a topping for other dishes.

Note: Remember to exercise caution while handling hot pans and sharp knives.
