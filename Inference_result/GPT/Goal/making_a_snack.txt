Action Planning for Making a Snack:

1. Gather the ingredients needed for the snack.
2. Prepare any necessary tools or equipment.
3. Follow a recipe or decide on the snack you want to make.
4. Measure and mix the ingredients according to the recipe.
5. Cook or assemble the snack as directed.
6. Plate or arrange the snack in a presentable manner.
7. Enjoy your delicious homemade snack!
