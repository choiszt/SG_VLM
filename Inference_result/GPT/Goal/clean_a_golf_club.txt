Action Planning to Clean a Golf Club:
1. Gather necessary cleaning supplies: a bucket, warm soapy water, a soft bristle brush, and a towel.
2. Fill the bucket with warm soapy water.
3. Dip the soft bristle brush into the soapy water.
4. Gently scrub the club head, paying attention to the grooves and face of the club.
5. Rinse the club head with clean water to remove any soap residue.
6. Dry the club head thoroughly with a towel.
7. Store the clean golf club in a safe and dry place until next use.
