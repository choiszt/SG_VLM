Action Planning for Scraping Snow off Vehicle:

1. Gather necessary tools: snow scraper, snow brush, and gloves.
2. Put on gloves to protect hands from cold and wetness.
3. Use the snow brush to remove loose snow from the vehicle's windows, roof, and hood.
4. Use the snow scraper to carefully remove the remaining snow and ice from the windshield and other windows.
5. Clear the snow and ice from the side mirrors and headlights using the snow brush or scraper.
6. Check for any remaining snow or ice on the vehicle and remove it using the appropriate tool.
7. After completing the scraping process, shake off any snow from the tools and store them in a safe place.

Note: Please ensure that the vehicle is parked in a safe and accessible location before starting the scraping process.
