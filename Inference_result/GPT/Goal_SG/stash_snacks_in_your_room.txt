Action Planning:

1. Determine the snacks you want to stash in your room.
2. Gather the selected snacks from the kitchen or pantry.
3. Find a suitable storage container or bag to hold the snacks.
4. Ensure the container or bag is clean and free from any debris.
5. Place the snacks neatly and organized in the container or bag.
6. Choose a suitable location in your room to store the snacks.
7. Carefully place the container or bag in the chosen location, ensuring it is easily accessible but out of sight if desired.

Note: Please make sure to follow any guidelines or rules set by your household regarding food storage in your room.
