Action Planning for clean_up_gasoline:

1. Ensure safety: Put on protective gloves and goggles to avoid any contact with gasoline.

2. Ventilate the area: Open windows and doors to allow fresh air to circulate and remove the smell of gasoline.

3. Absorb excess gasoline: Use absorbent material such as paper towels, cat litter, or sawdust to soak up any visible spills. Gently press down on the material to help absorb the gasoline.

4. Dispose of absorbent material: Carefully place the used absorbent material in a sealable plastic bag to prevent any further spills or leaks. Dispose of it according to local regulations for hazardous waste.

5. Clean the affected area: Use a mixture of dish soap and warm water to clean the area where the gasoline was spilled. Scrub gently with a cloth or sponge to remove any remaining residue.

6. Rinse the area: Rinse the cleaned area with clean water to remove any soap residue.

7. Properly store cleaning materials: Ensure that all cleaning materials, including gloves and goggles, are properly cleaned and stored in a safe place after use.
