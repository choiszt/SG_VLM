Action Planning for Taking Clothes out of Washer:

1. Open the lid or door of the washer.
2. Use a pair of gloves (optional) to protect your hands from wet clothes.
3. Remove the clothes from the washer and place them in a laundry basket or a clean surface.
4. Check for any remaining water in the washer and drain it if necessary.
5. Close the lid or door of the washer to prevent any accidents.
6. If desired, add fabric softener or dryer sheets to the laundry basket or surface where the clothes are placed.
7. Take the clothes to a drying area or transfer them to a dryer for further drying.

Remember to follow safety precautions and guidelines provided by the manufacturer when handling appliances and wet clothing.
