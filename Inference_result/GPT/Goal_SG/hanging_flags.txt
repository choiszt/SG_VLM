Planning for hanging flags:

1. Gather flags and necessary tools (hammer, nails, ladder).
2. Determine the location for hanging the flags.
3. Set up the ladder in the desired location.
4. Attach the first flag to the wall using a nail and hammer.
5. Step down from the ladder and adjust the position of the first flag if needed.
6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 for the remaining flags, ensuring they are evenly spaced and aligned.
7. Safely remove the ladder and store all tools and materials.

Note: Ensure to follow safety guidelines while using the ladder and handling tools.
