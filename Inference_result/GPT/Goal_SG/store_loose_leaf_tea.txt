Sure! Here is a simple action planning to store loose leaf tea:

1. Gather a clean, airtight container.
2. Open the packaging of the loose leaf tea.
3. Pour the loose leaf tea into the container.
4. Seal the container tightly to prevent moisture and air from entering.
5. Label the container with the type of tea and the date of storage.
6. Find a cool and dark place to store the container, away from direct sunlight and heat sources.
7. Remember to use the stored tea within its recommended expiration date for the best flavor.
