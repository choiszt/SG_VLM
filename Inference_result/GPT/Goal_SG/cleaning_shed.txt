Action Planning for cleaning_shed:

1. Gather necessary cleaning supplies such as a broom, dustpan, mop, bucket, and cleaning solution.
2. Remove any large debris or objects from the shed that may obstruct cleaning.
3. Sweep the floor of the shed to remove dust and dirt.
4. Use a damp mop or cloth with cleaning solution to wipe down any surfaces in the shed, such as shelves or workbenches.
5. Empty and clean any trash bins or containers inside the shed.
6. Check for any cobwebs or spider webs and remove them with a broom or duster.
7. Finally, ensure that all cleaning supplies are properly stored and the shed is left in a tidy and organized state.
