Action Planning for Putting Away Bicycles:

1. Identify a suitable storage location for the bicycles.
2. Clear any obstacles or clutter in the chosen storage area.
3. Ensure that the storage area is well-ventilated and accessible.
4. Gather any necessary tools or equipment, such as a bike stand or hooks.
5. Carefully lift each bicycle and place it in the storage area.
6. Secure the bicycles in place to prevent them from falling or getting damaged.
7. Double-check that all bicycles are properly stored and the storage area is safe.

Note: Depending on the specific storage area and requirements, some steps may vary.
