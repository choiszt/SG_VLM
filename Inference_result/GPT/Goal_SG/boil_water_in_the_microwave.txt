Action Planning to Boil Water in the Microwave:

1. Fill a microwave-safe container with water.
2. Place the container in the microwave.
3. Set the desired time for boiling the water.
4. Close the microwave door securely.
5. Start the microwave by pressing the appropriate button.
6. Monitor the microwave to ensure the water is boiling.
7. Carefully remove the container from the microwave using oven mitts or a towel.

Note: Please follow all safety precautions provided by the microwave manufacturer while using the appliance.
