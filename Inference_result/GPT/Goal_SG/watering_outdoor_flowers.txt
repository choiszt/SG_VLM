Action Planning for Watering Outdoor Flowers:
1. Gather watering can and fill it with water.
2. Put on appropriate outdoor clothing and shoes.
3. Walk outside to the garden or flower beds.
4. Identify the flowers or plants that need watering.
5. Slowly and evenly pour water onto the base of each plant, avoiding the leaves if possible.
6. Ensure each plant receives an adequate amount of water.
7. Return the watering can to its designated storage area.
