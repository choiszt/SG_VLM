Action Planning:
1. Gather necessary cleaning supplies such as a broom, dustpan, and cleaning solution.
2. Remove any large debris or dirt from the wood pallets using the broom and dustpan.
3. Mix the cleaning solution with water according to the instructions on the packaging.
4. Dip a cloth or sponge into the cleaning solution and scrub the wood pallets thoroughly, focusing on any stained or dirty areas.
5. Rinse the wood pallets with clean water to remove any residue from the cleaning solution.
6. Allow the wood pallets to air dry completely.
7. Optional: Apply a wood sealer or protectant to enhance the appearance and durability of the pallets.
