Action Plan to Make Cookie Dough:

1. Gather all the necessary ingredients: flour, sugar, butter, eggs, vanilla extract, baking soda, and salt.
2. Measure the required amount of flour, sugar, and butter according to the recipe.
3. Combine the measured flour, sugar, and butter in a mixing bowl.
4. Add eggs, vanilla extract, baking soda, and salt to the mixing bowl.
5. Mix all the ingredients together until well combined and a dough forms.
6. Once the dough is ready, cover it with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow it to firm up.
7. After refrigeration, the cookie dough is ready to be used for baking cookies.
