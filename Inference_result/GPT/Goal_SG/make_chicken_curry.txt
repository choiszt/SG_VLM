Action Plan to Make Chicken Curry:

1. Gather all the ingredients needed for chicken curry, such as chicken pieces, onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, spices (like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder), oil, salt, and water.
2. Prepare the chicken by washing it thoroughly and cutting it into small pieces if necessary.
3. Chop the onions, tomatoes, garlic, and ginger finely.
4. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the chopped onions, garlic, and ginger until they turn golden brown.
5. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are browned on all sides.
6. Add the chopped tomatoes and spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder) to the pan and mix well.
7. Pour in enough water to cover the chicken, bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the curry has thickened.

Remember to adjust the cooking time and spice levels according to your preferences. Enjoy your homemade chicken curry!
