Action Planning for making iced chocolate:

1. Gather ingredients: chocolate syrup, milk, ice cubes, and whipped cream.
2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
3. Pour chocolate syrup into the glass, about 2 tablespoons.
4. Add milk to the glass, filling it about Â¾ full.
5. Stir the mixture well to combine the syrup and milk.
6. Top the glass with whipped cream.
7. Serve and enjoy your homemade iced chocolate!
