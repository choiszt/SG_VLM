Action Planning for Cooking Food for Adults:

1. Decide on the dish to cook based on the preferences of the adults.
2. Make a list of ingredients needed for the dish.
3. Check the pantry and refrigerator for the availability of the required ingredients.
4. Go grocery shopping if any ingredients are missing.
5. Prepare the ingredients by washing, cutting, and measuring them as required.
6. Follow the recipe instructions to cook the dish.
7. Serve the cooked food to the adults.

Note: Please ensure to follow proper safety precautions while cooking, such as using oven mitts, turning off the stove after use, and handling sharp objects with care.
