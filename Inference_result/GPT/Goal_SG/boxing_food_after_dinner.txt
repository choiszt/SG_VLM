Action Planning:
1. Gather the necessary materials for boxing the food, such as food containers, plastic wrap, and labels.
2. Clear and clean the kitchen countertop or any other suitable area for boxing the food.
3. Separate the leftover food into individual portions or servings.
4. Place each portion of food into a food container, ensuring that the containers are properly sealed.
5. Label each food container with the name of the dish and the date it was prepared.
6. Wrap the food containers with plastic wrap to further secure and protect the food.
7. Store the boxed food in the refrigerator or freezer, depending on the type of food and desired storage duration.
