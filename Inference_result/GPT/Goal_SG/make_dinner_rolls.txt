Action Plan to Make Dinner Rolls:

1. Gather all the necessary ingredients for dinner rolls: flour, yeast, sugar, salt, butter, and water.
2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
3. Melt the butter and add it to the dry ingredients. Mix again.
4. Gradually add water to the mixture while stirring, until a soft dough forms.
5. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
6. Shape the dough into small rolls and place them on a greased baking sheet.
7. Let the rolls rise in a warm place for about an hour, or until they have doubled in size.

That's it! You now have a plan to make delicious dinner rolls. Enjoy your meal!
