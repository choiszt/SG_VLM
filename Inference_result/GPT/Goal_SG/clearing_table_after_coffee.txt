Action Planning for Clearing Table after Coffee:

1. Gather necessary cleaning supplies (dishcloth, sponge, cleaning spray).
2. Remove any remaining coffee cups or mugs from the table and place them in the sink.
3. Wipe down the table surface using the dishcloth and cleaning spray to remove any spills or stains.
4. Collect any used napkins or paper towels and dispose of them in the trash.
5. Check for any leftover crumbs or food on the table and use the sponge to wipe them away.
6. Inspect the surrounding area for any additional messes or spills and clean them accordingly.
7. Once the table is clean, return the cleaning supplies to their designated storage area.
