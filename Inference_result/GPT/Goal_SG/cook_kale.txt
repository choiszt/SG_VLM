Action Planning for Task: cook_kale

1. Gather ingredients: Obtain fresh kale leaves, olive oil, salt, and any desired additional seasonings or toppings.
2. Wash the kale: Rinse the kale leaves under cold water to remove any dirt or debris.
3. Remove the stems: Hold each kale leaf by the stem and use your fingers or a knife to strip off the leaves, discarding the stems.
4. Chop the kale: Stack the kale leaves and thinly slice them into bite-sized pieces.
5. Heat the pan: Place a frying pan or skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil.
6. Cook the kale: Add the chopped kale to the heated pan and saut√© for 3-5 minutes until the leaves are wilted and slightly crispy.
7. Season and serve: Sprinkle the cooked kale with salt and any desired additional seasonings or toppings, such as lemon juice, garlic, or parmesan cheese. Serve as a side dish or incorporate into other recipes as desired.
