Planning for making garlic mushrooms:

1. Gather the ingredients:
   - Mushrooms
   - Garlic cloves
   - Olive oil
   - Salt
   - Pepper
   - Parsley (optional)

2. Clean the mushrooms by wiping them with a damp cloth or rinsing them briefly under running water. Pat them dry with a paper towel.

3. Slice the mushrooms into thin, even slices.

4. Peel and mince the garlic cloves.

5. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil.

6. Add the minced garlic to the pan and saut√© for a minute until fragrant.

7. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pan and cook for about 5-7 minutes until they are tender and golden brown. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

(Optional) 8. If desired, sprinkle some chopped parsley over the mushrooms for added freshness and flavor.

Your garlic mushrooms are now ready to be enjoyed as a side dish or added to various recipes!
