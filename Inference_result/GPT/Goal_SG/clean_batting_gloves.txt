1. Gather necessary cleaning supplies such as mild soap, water, and a soft cloth.
2. Fill a sink or basin with warm water and add a small amount of mild soap.
3. Submerge the batting gloves in the soapy water and gently agitate them to remove dirt and grime.
4. Use the soft cloth to gently scrub any stained or soiled areas of the gloves.
5. Rinse the gloves thoroughly with clean water to remove any soap residue.
6. Squeeze out excess water from the gloves and gently reshape them.
7. Allow the gloves to air dry completely before using them again.
