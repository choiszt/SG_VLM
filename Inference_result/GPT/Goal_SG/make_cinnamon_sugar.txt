Action Planning for Making Cinnamon Sugar:

1. Gather the ingredients: cinnamon and sugar.
2. Take a clean bowl or container.
3. Measure the desired amount of sugar and pour it into the bowl.
4. Measure the desired amount of cinnamon and add it to the bowl.
5. Use a spoon or fork to mix the sugar and cinnamon together until well combined.
6. Taste the mixture and adjust the amount of cinnamon or sugar if needed.
7. Transfer the cinnamon sugar into an airtight container for storage.

Note: Remember to be careful while handling the ingredients and avoid any spills or accidents.
