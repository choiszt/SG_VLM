Action Planning for Buying Salad Greens:
1. Check the refrigerator to see if there are any salad greens available.
2. If there are no salad greens, make a shopping list with "salad greens" as an item.
3. Grab a reusable shopping bag and head to the grocery store.
4. In the produce section, locate the salad greens aisle or section.
5. Choose a fresh and crisp bag or bunch of salad greens.
6. Place the salad greens in the shopping bag.
7. Pay for the salad greens at the checkout counter and return home.
