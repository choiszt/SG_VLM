Action Planning for cleaning a golf club:

1. Gather necessary cleaning supplies such as a bucket, warm soapy water, a brush, and a towel.
2. Fill the bucket with warm soapy water.
3. Dip the brush into the soapy water and scrub the club head, paying special attention to the grooves.
4. Rinse the club head with clean water from the bucket to remove any soap residue.
5. Use the towel to dry the club head thoroughly.
6. Clean the grip of the golf club by wiping it down with a damp cloth.
7. Allow the golf club to air dry before storing it.
