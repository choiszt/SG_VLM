Planning for throwing out used napkins:

1. Gather all used napkins from around the house.
2. Place the used napkins in a small trash bag or garbage bin.
3. Tie the trash bag securely to prevent any spills or leaks.
4. Take the trash bag outside to the designated trash area or garbage bin.
5. Open the trash bin or lift the lid of the garbage bin.
6. Carefully deposit the trash bag into the bin.
7. Close the lid of the garbage bin securely.

Note: Make sure to wash your hands thoroughly after completing this task to maintain hygiene.
