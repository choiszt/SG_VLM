1. Identify the type of stain and determine the appropriate treatment method.
2. Blot the stain gently with a clean cloth or paper towel to remove any excess liquid or residue.
3. Check the care label on the stained item to ensure the treatment method will not damage the fabric.
4. Apply a stain remover or detergent directly to the stained area and let it sit for a few minutes to penetrate the stain.
5. Gently scrub the stained area using a soft brush or sponge to help loosen the stain.
6. Rinse the area with clean water to remove any remaining stain remover or detergent.
7. Launder the item according to the care instructions, either by hand or in a washing machine, to complete the stain removal process.
