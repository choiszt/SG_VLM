Action Planning for Cleaning a Loofah or Natural Sponge:

1. Fill a clean sink or basin with warm water.
2. Add a small amount of mild liquid soap or dish detergent to the water.
3. Place the loofah or natural sponge into the soapy water and let it soak for a few minutes.
4. Use your hands to gently squeeze and massage the loofah or sponge to help remove any dirt or debris.
5. Rinse the loofah or sponge thoroughly under running water to remove all soap residue.
6. Squeeze out any excess water and gently pat the loofah or sponge dry with a clean towel.
7. Hang the loofah or sponge in a well-ventilated area to air dry completely before using it again.

Note: It is important to clean and dry the loofah or natural sponge thoroughly to prevent the growth of bacteria.
