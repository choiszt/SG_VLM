Planning for making an iced espresso:

1. Gather the necessary ingredients: espresso coffee, ice cubes, milk, and sweetener (optional).
2. Brew a shot of espresso using an espresso machine or a stovetop espresso maker.
3. Let the espresso cool down for a few minutes.
4. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
5. Pour the cooled espresso over the ice cubes.
6. Add milk to taste. You can use regular milk, almond milk, or any other milk alternative.
7. If desired, add sweetener such as sugar or flavored syrup.

Enjoy your iced espresso!
