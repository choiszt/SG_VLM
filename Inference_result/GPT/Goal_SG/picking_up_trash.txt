Action Planning for picking_up_trash:

2. Grab a trash bag or bin.
3. Approach the trash and carefully pick it up.
4. Place the trash in the bag or bin.
5. Close the bag or secure the lid of the bin.

7. Clean and sanitize your hands after handling the trash.
